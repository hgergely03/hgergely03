About the project

The two-in-one app package features an application containing a countdown until our Sun becoming a red giant, alongside numerous interesting facts about its lifecycle. Its companion app, nApp: VR, is a virtual reality experience that lets users explore the Sun's evolution both across time and space.

Development

Being visually appealing is cardinal in the app. Flutter is a great choice to build such a UI. With its help, information can be easily displayed in a clean and intuitive way. During the development, both designing the user interface and its development were part of my duties. With the app duo in 2021, the developer group received second place in the Szegedi Innovatív Informatika Verseny.
